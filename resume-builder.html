How to export your LinkedIn profile (takes ~2 min):

  1. Go to linkedin.com → click your profile photo (top right)
  2. Click Settings & Privacy
  3. Click Data Privacy → then "Get a copy of your data"
  4. Select "The works" → click Request archive
  5. LinkedIn emails you a ZIP file — usually within 10 minutes
  6. Unzip it → find the file called Profile.pdf → upload it below
